---

- 开放端口
~~~
firewall-cmd --add-port=2375/tcp --permanent
firewall-cmd --reload
~~~

- 关闭端口
~~~
firewall-cmd --remove-port=80/tcp --permanent
firewall-cmd --reload
~~~

- 列出已开放端口
~~~
firewall-cmd --zone=public --list-ports
~~~
